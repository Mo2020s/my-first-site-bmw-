BMW I8

BMW i8 is a plug-in hybrid sports car known for its futuristic design, combining a three-cylinder gasoline engine and an electric motor for impressive performance and efficiency. It offers all-wheel drive, accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in around 4 seconds, and can operate in electric-only mode for short distances.

BMW IX
